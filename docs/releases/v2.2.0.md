# v2.2.0 — Brief/spec expansion + Stage 7 auto-fold

Closes the gap between "what the brief says the feature needs" and
"what the pipeline actually captures" at Stage 1. Also removes a
redundant PM step on the clean-sheet test path.

## Highlights

### New required brief sections (full pipeline + `/hotfix`)

1. Rollback plan
2. Feature flag / rollout strategy
3. Data migration safety
4. Observability requirements (one signal per acceptance criterion)
5. SLO / error-budget impact
6. Cost impact

Lighter tracks can condense into a single `## Risk notes` line when
every dimension is genuinely trivial.

### New required design-spec section

§6 "Observability instrumentation" names the specific metric/log/trace
primitive for each acceptance criterion, plus SLI references where SLOs
are named.

### Stage 7 auto-fold

When Stage 6 maps every acceptance criterion 1:1 to a passing test,
the orchestrator writes Stage 7 directly with `"auto_from_stage_06":
true` (no PM invocation). `/hotfix` always requires manual sign-off;
ambiguous mappings and user-requested manual reviews also skip the
auto-fold.

### Canonical templates

- `docs/brief-template.md` — full form, condensed form, worked example
- `docs/design-spec-template.md` — with a §6 worked example

## Breaking

- **Legacy briefs fail Stage 1 `required_sections_complete`** on the
  full track until the six risk sections are added. A one-word
  "None" is valid for genuinely-empty dimensions.
- **Stage 7 gate `agent` field** can now be `"orchestrator"` (auto-fold
  path) or `"pm"` (manual path). Filter by `auto_from_stage_06` if
  the author matters.
- **New `required_sections_complete` field** on Stage 1 gates.
